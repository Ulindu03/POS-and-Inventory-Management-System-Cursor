## Sidebar Functions (Simple English)

- **Dashboard**: Shows the main summary of your business (sales, orders, products, etc.).
- **Sales**: Make new sales, see past sales, and manage receipts.
- **Inventory**: View, add, or update products in your store.
- **Returns**: Handle product returns from customers.
- **Suppliers**: Manage supplier details and contacts.
- **Customers**: See and manage customer information.
- **Reports/Analytics**: See charts and stats about sales, products, staff, and deliveries.
- **Users**: Add, edit, or remove staff/admin users. Set roles and permissions.
- **Settings**: Change system settings, language, and preferences.
- **Logout**: Sign out of the system.

> The sidebar helps you quickly go to any part of the POS system.

## Dashboard Panel (Simple English)

- **Revenue**: Shows total money earned in the selected period.
- **Orders**: Number of sales/orders made.
- **Products**: How many products are in your inventory.
- **Low/Out of Stock**: Products that are running low or are out of stock.
- **Revenue Trend**: Line chart showing how your sales change over time.
- **Top Products by Revenue**: Bar chart of best-selling products.
- **Inventory Value by Category**: Pie chart showing value of each product category.
- **Staff Revenue**: Bar chart showing how much each staff member sold.
- **Delivery Performance**: Stats about deliveries (total, completed, damaged, etc.).

> The dashboard gives you a quick look at your business health and helps you spot trends fast.
